Diesel, The Cat

About Diesel

Here are a few facts about my cat, Diesel:

  • He is a cat.
  • He is black.
  • He is often sick.
  • He stinks.
  • He loves to cuddle and beg for chicken.
  • He is the best cat in the world

Care Instructions

Things to know when cat-sitting Diesel:

  • Feed him one scoop of dry food at 11AM and another scoop at 11PM.
  • Give him a pill inside a pill pocket before feeding.
  • Wiggle his toy for him for a few minutes after he eats.
  • Make sure his water cup is always full.
  • Tell him he is a good boy and give him pets.
